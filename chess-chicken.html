About

Tucked into a laneway at the heart of Shida Night Market, Chess Chicken has earned a devoted following for one reason: the cheese pull. Their signature fried chicken is sliced open, packed with a generous load of molten mozzarella, then fried to golden perfection — a cross-section moment that's equal parts chaotic and beautiful.

The menu is tight and intentional — fried chicken in several cuts, taro balls, sweet potato puffs, and herb-dusted fries — letting the kitchen focus on doing a few things exceptionally well. Expect crispy skin, juicy meat, and cheese that stretches further than you'd believe.
