Why we exist

Taipei's food scene is extraordinary. Finding it shouldn't be this hard.

We've all stood on a street in Da'an at 7pm, starving, watching the same five tourist restaurants loop through Google Maps — while 50 metres away a family-run noodle shop that's been feeding this neighbourhood for 30 years has zero online presence. That drives us absolutely crazy.

Taipei deserves better. Its restaurants deserve better. We built Tasty Taipei to fix that — one restaurant at a time, district by district, until every great meal in this city is findable by everyone.

“The best meal our team ever had in Taipei was in a place with no English sign, no menu online, and no reviews anywhere. We almost walked straight past it. That can never happen again — for anyone.”

How it works

We do everything. Restaurants just keep cooking.

1
We walk in, we eat, we say helloEvery restaurant on Tasty Taipei has been personally visited by our team. We eat there first — because if the food isn't great, it's not going on the platform. Simple as that.
2
We build their full page within 48 hoursPhotos, complete menu in 5 languages, hours, location, booking and delivery buttons — all built by us. The owner's job is to keep cooking great food.
3
Tourists, expats, and locals discover themVisitors from France, Japan, the US — they search on Tasty Taipei and find the restaurant in their language, with a real menu and a working reservation button.
4
We come back with results — real numbersAfter 4 weeks we return with an actual report: page views, clicks, visitor nationalities. No fluff. Just proof it's working — and then we keep improving it.
