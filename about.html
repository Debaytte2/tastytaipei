About Tasty Taipei

Built by someone who got lost looking for lunch

A restaurant discovery platform made for Taipei — honest, simple, and built from the street up.

The story

Why this exists

Taipei has an extraordinary food scene — night market staples, family-run beef noodle shops, hole-in-the-wall ramen joints, bakeries older than most restaurants in the world. But if you're a tourist, an expat, or even a local in a new district, finding them is harder than it should be.

Google Maps surfaces the same 10 famous spots. Travel blogs go stale. And most small restaurants don't have the time or budget to manage an online presence at all.

"The best bowl of noodles I ever had was in a place with no English sign, no menu online, and no reviews anywhere. I almost walked past it."

Tasty Taipei is an attempt to fix that — giving small Taipei restaurants a proper digital home, and giving people a better way to find the real food in this city.

How it works

Simple for restaurants, useful for diners

1
We visit, you approve We come to your restaurant, eat something, take photos, and build your page — no forms to fill, no tech required on your end.
2
Your page goes live in 48 hours Menu, photos, hours, location, delivery options, reviews — all in English and Mandarin, with more languages on the way.
3
Diners discover you Tourists, expats, and locals use Tasty Taipei to find restaurants by cuisine, neighbourhood, price, and service type.
4
We show you the results After four weeks, we come back with a report — page views, clicks, actions. No surprises, no pressure.
Where we are

Launching now — in Taipei

Tasty Taipei launched in April 2026. We're starting with a small number of restaurants, building real relationships with owners, and growing district by district.

We're not a VC-backed app with a hundred engineers. We're a small team that cares about Taipei's food culture and thinks local restaurants deserve better tools. Every restaurant on this platform has been visited in person.

Live and active — Taipei, Taiwan
